Storage Polysaccharides: A Comparison

Starch 🌱

How plants store sugar — easy for us to digest.

Glycogen 🏋️‍♂️

How our bodies store sugar in the liver and muscles.

Cellulose 🥦

Found in vegetables. We can’t digest it, but it helps keep our bowels healthy.
